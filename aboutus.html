About Us!

We are first-year IT students from CTU-Daanbantayan who love taking photos. Our group started simple—friends with cameras, capturing moments around us. We’re still learning, still growing, and still exploring our style. We take pictures because it’s fun, creative, and lets us see the world differently. This is our little space to share what we shoot and what we enjoy.

 As we start our journey in the world of technology, we support each other in learning, exploring, and growing. Whether it’s helping one another with schoolwork, laughing over small things, or facing challenges together, we make sure no one is left behind. 

Our friendship is built on trust, respect, and good vibes. Every day becomes more meaningful because we know we have each other—inside and outside the classroom. 

We may just be at the beginning of our BSIT path, but we’re excited for the many lessons, achievements, and memories we’ll create as a group. Together, we move forward, united by friendship and a shared goal to succeed.
